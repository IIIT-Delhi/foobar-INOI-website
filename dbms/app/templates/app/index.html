CS Teachers Training Program 2015

1st August - 16th August 2015 (Saturdays)



About the Workshop

IIIT Delhi is organizing a workshop for Computer Science teachers in schools in the NCR region. The workshop aims to provide school teachers with pedagogical tools that may help improve the teaching of Computer Science. A second objective of the workshop is to provide a forum for teachers to exchange ideas for projects, and discuss technological issues. The workshop will start with following the NCERT syllabus, covering basic data structures, algorithms and programming concepts. The workshop will also include some advanced topics, as well as a forum for discussion on future careers in Computer Science and related fields. The workshop will be conducted by faculty from IIT Delhi, IIIT Delhi, Delhi University and engineers from Google. Each session of the workshop will consist of a lecture session followed by a programming lab. Registration for the workshop is free, but mandatory.

The teachers or schools who wish to attend the workshop must register here.


Objectives

1. Improve understanding of core concepts of computer science.
2. Provide a forum to discuss pedagogical issues.
4. Learn about applications of Computer Science.


Instructors

Faculty from IIT Delhi, IIIT Delhi, Delhi University and engineers from Google.
